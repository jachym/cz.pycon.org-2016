PyCon CZ aims to be the largest annual gathering for the Python community in the Czech Republic.
It's focused on honoring and supporting awesome people teaching, learning and innovating with Python in Czech Republic and surrounding EU.

Talks 28-29 October

Two days, three parallel tracks, four keynotes and 30+ awesome talks.

Sprints & workshops 30 October

Six workshops in two blocks and sprinting all day long. Hack on open-source projects together with other Python community members.
